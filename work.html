Let's take a look at the travel recommendations!

South Africa

Cape Town, a big city in South Africa famous for the beautiful view of a Table Mountain(seen on an aerial cableway) and great food(Bunny chow,Braai,Koeksister).

China

Beijing, a beautiful city in China with the marvelous attractions to see such as Freat Wall Of China,Temple of Heaven,Summer palace and Forbidden City. It is also one of the oldest cities with over 3000 years of history.

South Korea

Jeju Island, It has one of the most beautiful ocean views such as the Hamdeok Beach. It is a beautiful place to relax and eat good food.

Japan

Kyoto, a beautifulplace surrounded by rich natural beauty. Kyoto is famous for its many temples and also the quiet neighborhoods. It has delicious food to enjoy(Yudofu,Hamo,Tsukemono).
